Welcome to MAID

Welcome to the official website of Maine Against Impaired Driving. Our goal is to raise awareness about the dangers of impaired driving and make our roads safer for everyone.

About Us

Mission Statement: The Maine Against Impaired Driving (MAID) mission is to lower the number of annual impaired drivers and keep the roads of Maine a safer place.

Vision Statement: Making the state of Maine a safer place by eliminating impaired driving and fostering responsible choices.
